Music.

A modern, privacy-focused open-source Android music player built with Jetpack Compose. Seamlessly play anything from YouTube Music, ad-free.

INSTALL RAAZI 1.0 GITHUB REPO

Privacy

Privacy-First

No account required, no tracking, and zero data collection. Your listening habits stay on your device.

Library

YouTube Music

Search and play any song from the massive YouTube Music library without premium subscriptions.

Experience

Background Play

Keep the music alive while multi-tasking or with your screen locked. Full media control support.

Technical Documentation

Tech Stack

Language Kotlin
UI Framework Jetpack Compose
Architecture MVVM / Clean Arch
Playback Media3 (ExoPlayer)
Database Room Persistence
Extraction NewPipe Extractor
